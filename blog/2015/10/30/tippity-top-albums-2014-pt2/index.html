Tippity Top Albums 2014 (Pt2)

Oct 30th, 2015

I have had quite a bit of time to think about what I would like to write about for my latest blog and I feel for some sort of completeness that I need to wrap up my album of 2014. As it has been such a long delay that works that I had planned to review has changed somewhat.

Hundred Waters - The Moon Rang Like A Bell

Head-Over-Heels

For me pretty 2014 was an exciting year, I moved to down from the Scottish east coast to the bright lights (on the super efficient public transport) of London. Somewhere along the way I came across the tract Murmurs and subsequently their record. I fell head-over-heels in love with Cavity, from the first listen. It is something rather special
